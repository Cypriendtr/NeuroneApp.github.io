Here is some information about the Hodgkin and Huxley model :

This model originated in the 1950s from the work of the two researchers, Hodgkin and Huxley, for which they were awarded the Nobel Prize in Physiology and Medicine in 1963. In their article published in 1952, they studied the generation of action potentials in the axon of a giant squid.
